The Avenues Mall

The Avenues Mall is the largest complex and commercial shopping center in Kuwait .. The complex is located in Al-Rai Industrial Area along the Fifth Ring Road from the south and Al-Ghazali Highway from the east. In 2013 it won the gold award for "Best Shopping Center 2013" in the Middle East and North Africa.


Architectural Design

The architectural design of the complex is considered one of the most beautiful architectural and engineering designs in the State of Kuwait, as its exterior is distinguished by its modern style and surrounded by water fountains on each side, and its interior design is characterized by large areas and the many fountains scattered throughout the complex.
